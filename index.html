Our Story

A Sweet Escape
In The Heart Of Wayanad

Nestled among the lush green hills of Padinjarathara, Edhini Honey Villa is more than a place to sleep — it is a living experience of Wayanad's warmth, beauty, and authenticity. Every corner of this property breathes nature: the mist-wrapped mornings, the golden light through the canopy, the sound of water and birdsong.

We named our home after the sweetness we find in every moment here. The honey-gold light at dusk. The slow, gentle pace of life. The warmth of hosting families, couples, and solo travellers who leave as old friends. Come and discover what makes Wayanad truly special.

The Beauty Around You

Wayanad —
Nature's Masterpiece

The Western Ghats cradle Wayanad in a permanent embrace of green. From the mist that softens every morning to the amber light that gilds the forest at dusk, nature here doesn't just surround you — it speaks to you.

Padinjarathara is one of Wayanad's quieter corners — away from the tourist rush yet close to everything that makes this district extraordinary. Walk out our gate and you're already inside the experience.
